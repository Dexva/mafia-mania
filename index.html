A Machi Koro spin-off by CS5-J (SY '22-'23)

A city of opportunity and greed — welcome to New Apolinar City, mandamento.

Your family is a distinguished group of mafia members, with your father being one of the best dons that all of the underground has ever seen. After your father mysteriously passed away, you were sent by your organization to take over the world’s most luxurious city, New Apolinar City. However, due to the city’s insane population of wealth and power, it’s only normal that other mafia organizations would want to grasp its power, too.

Mafia Mania is a game of control, wherein you attempt to take over the five most powerful landmarks in the city before the other three to five other mafia organizations do.

Power is essential in New Apolinar City, but having supreme control will make you god. What are you waiting for, mandamento?
